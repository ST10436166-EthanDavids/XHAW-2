Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I register for courses?

    You can register by visiting the Calculate Fees page to choose your courses and submit your information.

  • What discounts are available?

    We offer discounts based on the number of courses you select: 5% for two courses, 10% for three, and 15% for more than three.

  • What are the payment methods?

    We accept EFT payments and cash at our Johannesburg office. Please contact us for more details.

  • What is the duration of each course?

    The duration varies depending on the course: Six-Month Courses last for six months, while Six-Week Courses are completed in six weeks.

  • Are there any prerequisites for the courses?

    Most courses have no prerequisites, but some may require basic skills or knowledge. Please refer to the course details for specific requirements.

  • Is there a certification upon completion?

    Yes, all students receive a certificate upon successful completion of their course, which can enhance their job prospects.

  • What if I miss a class?

    If you miss a class, please contact your instructor to discuss options for making up the session. We encourage students to attend all classes to maximize learning.

  • Can I transfer my registration to another course?

    Yes, transfers are possible under certain conditions. Please contact our office for more information on how to proceed.

  • How can I contact support for questions?

    You can reach us via our Contact Us page, where you can fill out a contact form or find our email and phone details.

  • Are there any age restrictions for enrolling in the courses?

    Generally, there are no age restrictions, but some courses may have recommended age limits. Check specific course details for more information.
