First Aid Course Overview

This comprehensive First Aid course is designed to equip participants with the essential skills and knowledge required to respond effectively in emergency situations. Throughout the six-month learnership, students will engage in both theoretical and practical training, covering fundamental first aid practices. The focus will be on emergency response and life-saving techniques, ensuring that participants feel confident managing critical situations at home, work, or in public spaces.

Course Structure

The course is structured into modules, allowing participants to progressively build their skills. Key components include:

  • Module 1: Introduction to First Aid - Basic concepts, principles, and legal considerations.
  • Module 2: CPR and Rescue Breathing - Techniques for adults, children, and infants.
  • Module 3: Wound Management - Cleaning, dressing, and treating various types of wounds.
  • Module 4: Managing Medical Emergencies - Identifying and responding to conditions such as strokes, heart attacks, and allergic reactions.
  • Module 5: Handling Injuries - Techniques for managing fractures, sprains, and burns.
  • Module 6: Practical Assessments - Hands-on practice sessions to ensure competency in skills learned.

Course Fee: R1500

Key Skills Covered:

  • Emergency response techniques, including CPR
  • Wound care, bleeding management, and bandaging
  • Treating burns, fractures, and other injuries
  • Handling life-threatening medical emergencies
  • Legal responsibilities of first aiders
  • Effective communication during emergencies
  • Using first aid kits and emergency supplies

Who Should Attend:

This course is ideal for anyone interested in learning basic to advanced first aid, whether for personal knowledge or professional application. It is particularly beneficial for:

  • Parents and caregivers
  • Teachers and coaches
  • Workplace health and safety officers
  • Community volunteers
  • Individuals pursuing a career in healthcare

Benefits of Completing the Course:

Participants will gain confidence in their ability to handle emergencies and may also enhance their employability in various fields. Successful completion of the course provides participants with a certification, recognizing their proficiency in first aid skills. This certification can be valuable for job applications in healthcare, education, and other sectors.
