Our Six-Month Learnerships

  • First Aid - R1500

    Learn essential first aid skills, including CPR, wound care, and emergency response techniques. This course prepares you to handle medical emergencies effectively and confidently.

  • Sewing - R1500

    Master the art of sewing with practical lessons on machine operation, fabric selection, and pattern creation. By the end of the course, you'll be able to create your own garments and repairs.

  • Landscaping - R1500

    Gain hands-on experience in garden design, plant care, and landscape maintenance. This course covers everything from soil preparation to sustainable gardening practices, equipping you with skills for a variety of landscaping tasks.

  • Life Skills - R1500

    Develop essential life skills, including financial literacy, time management, and communication techniques. This course is designed to enhance personal and professional effectiveness in daily life.

These courses are aimed at providing advanced skills over a longer period to ensure comprehensive learning.
