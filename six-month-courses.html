Our Six-Month Learnerships

Our six-month learnerships are designed to provide in-depth knowledge and hands-on experience in various fields. Each course ensures comprehensive learning and practical skills, preparing participants for real-world applications.

First Aid - R1500

This First Aid course equips participants with essential life-saving skills. The purpose of this course is to prepare individuals to respond effectively in emergencies, manage wounds, perform CPR, and handle various medical emergencies with confidence.

Key Skills: Emergency response, CPR, wound care, burn treatment.

Sewing - R1500

This Sewing course is aimed at individuals looking to gain practical sewing skills, from basic machine operation to garment construction. The purpose of this course is to teach participants how to create clothing and make alterations, with a focus on practical application.

Key Skills: Garment construction, sewing techniques, fabric selection.

Landscaping - R1500

The Landscaping course is designed for aspiring garden designers and landscape professionals. The purpose of this course is to give participants hands-on experience in garden design, plant care, and sustainable landscaping techniques.

Key Skills: Garden design, plant care, sustainable practices.

Life Skills - R1500

The Life Skills course focuses on essential personal and professional skills. The purpose of this course is to equip participants with abilities such as time management, financial literacy, and effective communication to succeed in both personal and career endeavors.

Key Skills: Financial literacy, communication, time management.
