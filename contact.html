Our Initiative

The SME initiative, Empowering the Nation, was founded by Precious Radebe to provide skills training for domestic workers and gardeners. Precious was motivated by the lack of upskilling opportunities available to her parents and elderly relatives. This training school aims to enhance job marketability, enable higher pay, and support entrepreneurial ventures for domestic workers and gardeners. Our programs help individuals become more skilled, allowing them to start their own businesses or provide more specialized services to employers.
