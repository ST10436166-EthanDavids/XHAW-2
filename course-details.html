Available Courses

1. First Aid

This course provides essential skills in first aid and CPR. You'll learn how to respond to medical emergencies and provide basic life support.

2. Sewing

Our sewing course covers basic techniques to advanced garment construction. Participants will learn how to sew garments and home textiles.

3. Landscaping

This course teaches landscaping design principles, plant care, and maintenance techniques to enhance outdoor spaces.

4. Life Skills

Focusing on personal development, this course covers communication skills, time management, and conflict resolution.

5. Child Minding

Participants learn essential childcare skills, including safety, hygiene, and engaging children in age-appropriate activities.

6. Cooking

This course covers basic to advanced cooking techniques, meal planning, and nutrition to equip participants with culinary skills.

7. Garden Maintenance

Participants will learn about plant care, lawn maintenance, and sustainable gardening practices.
