Our Six-Week Short Courses

Our six-week short courses are designed to provide practical skills in a condensed format, perfect for those who want to learn quickly and effectively. Each course is tailored to meet the needs of participants, ensuring that you gain relevant knowledge and hands-on experience.

  • Child Minding - R750

    Course Duration: 6 weeks (1 session per week, 2 hours per session)

    Prerequisites: None

    Key Topics Covered:

    • Understanding child development stages
    • Age-appropriate activities and games
    • Safety and emergency procedures
    • Effective communication with children
    • Creating a nurturing environment

    This course provides practical skills for responsible and nurturing child care.

  • Cooking - R750

    Course Duration: 6 weeks (1 session per week, 3 hours per session)

    Prerequisites: None

    Key Topics Covered:

    • Meal planning and preparation
    • Basic cooking techniques and food safety
    • Creating a variety of dishes (appetizers, main courses, desserts)
    • Nutrition and healthy eating
    • Cooking on a budget

    Acquire essential cooking skills with lessons on meal planning, basic techniques, and recipe execution.

  • Garden Maintenance - R750

    Course Duration: 6 weeks (1 session per week, 2 hours per session)

    Prerequisites: None

    Key Topics Covered:

    • Plant selection and care
    • Pest management and organic gardening
    • Seasonal garden tasks (planting, pruning, harvesting)
    • Creating a sustainable garden
    • Basic landscaping techniques

    This course is perfect for those looking to maintain a beautiful and healthy garden with practical, easy-to-follow techniques.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of these courses, participants will:

  • Gain practical skills and knowledge applicable in real-life situations.
  • Enhance their employability in various fields, including childcare and culinary arts.
  • Build confidence in their ability to manage tasks independently.
